## 2) What AMC Is
AMC is a private, case-based decision brief service for professionals at meaningful crossroads. It is not generic coaching or generic AI advice; it is a structured read of decision tension, constraints, and defensibility.

## 3) Who It Is For
- Experienced professionals facing consequential career decisions
- People balancing compensation, stability, family constraints, and long-term positioning
- Clients who want a structural decision read, not broad motivational guidance

## 4) How It Works
1. Submit intake context, constraints, options, and timing signals.
2. Receive an AMC structural report (single-path or comparative).
3. Choose delivery mode: Essential (report only) or Executive (report + 7-day chatbot access).

## 5) What the Report Covers
- Executive structural reading of the core decision tension
- External pressure context (market, competition, economic, transition)
- Five-factor structural scorecard and risk diagnosis
- Value, mobility, and temperament interpretation layers
- Decision conditions for validation, readiness, support, and commitment
