## 2) What AMC Is
AMC is a private career architecture service for decisions where stakes, constraints, and timing matter. Instead of starting from motivation or generic advice, AMC starts from structure: external pressure, internal burden, path trade-offs, and commitment conditions. The output is a decision brief, not a coaching script.

## 3) Who It Is For
- Experienced professionals at meaningful transition points
- Operators and leaders balancing compensation, stability, family constraints, and long-term positioning
- People facing structural tension between two paths, or evaluating whether one path is sustainable
- Clients who want a disciplined decision read rather than broad inspirational guidance

## 4) How It Works
1. Intake: You submit career context, constraints, options, and timing signals.
2. Structural Report: AMC produces a structured DOCX decision brief with diagnostic, comparative/single-path, and condition-based layers.
3. Delivery Tier Selection:
- Essential: Report only
- Executive: Report + 7-day chatbot access for follow-up interpretation and clarification
4. Optional Follow-Up: For Executive tier, clients can pressure-test implications and assumptions during the 7-day window.

## 5) What the Report Covers
- Executive Reading: A concise structural framing of the case and core tension
- External Snapshot: Market, competition, economic, and transition pressure context
- Structural Scorecard and Diagnosis: Five-factor risk reading and burden concentration
- Value / Mobility / Temperament Layers: How path value logic, movement architecture, and behavioral governance interact
- Decision Conditions: Validation, readiness, support, and commitment thresholds
- Operational Follow-Through: Exploration, execution, and watchlist elements where applicable

## 8) FAQ Draft (10)

### 1. Is AMC career coaching?
No. AMC is a structural decision-read service, not a coaching program.

### 2. Does AMC tell me what to choose?
AMC does not choose for you. It clarifies structure, tension, and commitment conditions.

### 3. Who is AMC most useful for?
Professionals facing high-impact decisions with real constraints and trade-offs.

### 4. What do I receive?
A structured AMC report. Executive tier also includes 7-day chatbot access.

### 5. Can AMC handle comparative decisions?
Yes. Comparative cases are a core mode and include structured side-by-side reading.

### 6. Can AMC handle single-path cases?
Yes. AMC can assess one path for structural sustainability and commitment defensibility.

### 7. Is AMC only for job changes?
No. It is for broader career-structure decisions, including stay/transition and timing questions.

### 8. What if my case has incomplete information?
AMC still provides a structured read, while signaling assumptions and conditions that require validation.

### 9. Can AMC be used in different languages?
AMC is prepared for Korean, English, and Chinese delivery workflows.

### 10. Is AMC a long-term advisory subscription?
No. AMC is case-based. Executive adds a defined 7-day follow-up window.
